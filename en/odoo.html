What is Odoo?

Odoo is an all-in-one business management platform that centralises your accounting, CRM, inventory, HR, and marketing in one easy-to-use system.

With flexible modules and scalable features, Odoo helps companies save time, reduce errors, and accelerate growth. At Bodoo, we specialise in tailored Odoo solutions for creative entrepreneurs and growing businesses in Belgium.

Frequently asked questions

01.
Is Odoo suitable for Belgian VAT returns?

Yes, Odoo is fully aligned with Belgian VAT regulations. The software includes preconfigured VAT codes, Intrastat reporting, and periodic VAT filings that meet the standards of the Belgian tax authorities.
It’s ideal for freelancers and SMEs looking for reliable Odoo accounting.

02.
Can I import my existing accounting data into Odoo?

Absolutely. Bodoo Accountants migrates your full accounting history from tools like Exact Online, Sage, or Winbooks so you can switch to a modern, digital solution without losing any data.
Choose a smooth Odoo implementation with zero data loss.

03.
How much does Odoo accounting software cost for an SME?

Pricing depends on your specific needs, but Odoo is one of the most cost-effective solutions on the market.
Bodoo Accountants helps you choose exactly what you need with no unnecessary extras.

04.
Which modules can I connect to Odoo Accounting?

Odoo offers a complete ecosystem including sales, inventory management, HR, e-commerce, CRM, and more.
Thanks to seamless integrations, you work faster and more efficiently, all within one system built for digital growth.
