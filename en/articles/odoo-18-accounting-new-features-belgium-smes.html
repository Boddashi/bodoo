Odoo 18 accounting: 9 new features Belgian SMEs can’t ignore

Discover how the latest Odoo updates simplify your VAT returns, invoicing and compliance.
With the release of Odoo 18, the platform introduces a series of improvements that make administration faster, easier and more efficient for Belgian SMEs.
The accounting module in particular received a major upgrade, with features that focus on automation, VAT compliance and real-time reporting.

In this article, we list the 9 most important new accounting features in Odoo 18 that every Belgian entrepreneur should know.

Why Odoo 18 matters for Belgian SMEs

Belgian legislation around accounting and VAT continues to evolve.
Companies must report faster, invoice more accurately, and collaborate more digitally with clients and suppliers.

Odoo Accounting responds to this with:

  1. Automatic VAT returns that follow Belgian rules
  2. Real-time dashboards for cash flow and profit
  3. Integrated Peppol e‑invoicing and bank syncing

For SMEs in construction, logistics, consulting, retail, marketing or IT, this means more control, fewer mistakes and a more professional approach.

The 9 best new accounting features in Odoo 18

1. Improved VAT codes tailored to Belgian rules

Odoo 18 supports all specific Belgian VAT regimes, including reverse-charge, intra-community deliveries and exemptions. Reports now align automatically with the declaration format of the FPS Finance.

2. Peppol e‑invoicing built-in by default

Send and receive electronic invoices directly via the Peppol network. No need for extra tools. Perfect for working with government clients or large businesses.

3. Automatic recognition of incoming invoices

Thanks to built-in AI, Odoo now scans PDF invoices and recognizes amounts, suppliers, VAT codes and booking rules. Less manual work and faster processing.

4. One-click smart bank reconciliation

Odoo suggests automatic links between bank transactions and invoices. With a single click, you can confirm dozens of payments and receipts—saving hours every month.

5. Cash flow forecasting based on real and expected data

The new cash flow report combines outstanding invoices, recurring expenses and bank balances. Ideal for predicting surpluses or shortfalls in advance.

6. Budgeting per project, client or department

Set budgets for each project or team, and automatically track deviations. Useful for consultancy, IT and marketing firms that report by client.

7. Visual improvements to the accounting screen

New views, colors and intuitive buttons make navigation easier, even for non-accountants. Get faster access to action buttons and filters.

8. Automated monthly and quarterly reports

Schedule your accounting reports in advance. Odoo generates and sends them automatically to your accountant or management team.

9. Extended audit trail and control tools

Every change in accounting data is now logged with timestamp, user and reason. Essential for SMEs aiming to meet audit obligations and ensure transparency.

What this means for your business in Belgium

These new features allow you to:

  1. File VAT returns faster
  2. Reduce errors in invoice processing
  3. Collaborate more professionally with accountants
  4. Gain real-time insights into profit and cash position

Whether you're a startup or an established company, Odoo 18 Accounting offers a solid upgrade for every Belgian SME.

Conclusion: upgrade to Odoo 18 with the right support

Switching to Odoo 18 is more than a software update.
It’s a chance to streamline your accounting, improve compliance and professionalize your financial operations.
