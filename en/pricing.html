Frequently asked questions  

01.
What is the difference between the plans?

Each plan meets different business needs, from legal compliance and tax filing to strategic financial guidance and personalized Odoo solutions. The higher the plan, the more comprehensive the support and insights.

02.
Are the prices fixed?

Yes, the Basic and Standard plans have fixed monthly rates. The Premium plan is based on a custom quote depending on your specific needs.

03.
Is support included in each plan?

Yes. All plans include support via email and phone. Higher-tier plans offer strategic sessions and more in-depth guidance.

04.
Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or modify your plan anytime as your business evolves. Just contact us and we’ll ensure a smooth transition.

05.
Is Belgian VAT filing included?

Yes. All plans include preparation and submission of VAT returns, personal income tax and withholding tax, fully compliant with Belgian legislation.
